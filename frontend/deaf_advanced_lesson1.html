The Right Angle — 90°
The right angle (90°) is always opposite the longest side.
It is the defining feature of a right triangle.
Key Properties at a Glance

The Hypotenuse
The hypotenuse (c) is always opposite the 90° angle.
It is ALWAYS longer than either leg individually.
