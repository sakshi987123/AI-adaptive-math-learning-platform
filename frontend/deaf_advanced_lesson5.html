APPLICATION 1 · SAFETY & CONSTRUCTION
🪜 LADDER AGAINST A WALL
①
Known: wall height a = 8 m, ground distance b = 6 m
②
Use formula:
c² = a² + b²
③
Substitute:
c² = 8² + 6² = 64 + 36
④
Solve:
c² = 100
∴ c = √100 = 10 m
APPLICATION 2 · NAVIGATION & DISTANCE
🧭 SHORTEST DISTANCE
①
Two roads form right angle. East: b = 130 m, North: a = 150 m
②
Shortcut = hypotenuse
c² = 130² + 150²
③
= 16 900 + 22 500
= 39 400
④
c = √39 400
≈ 198.5 m saved!
APPLICATION 3 · ARCHITECTURE & BUILDING
🏗️ CHECKING A RIGHT ANGLE
①
Room: width a = 12 m, height b = 9 m. Find diagonal to check corners.
②
c² = 12² + 9²
= 144 + 81 = 225
③
c = √225 = 15 m
④
If both diagonals = 15 m →
Corners are 90°! ✓
APPLICATION 4 · COORDINATE GEOMETRY
📍 DISTANCE BETWEEN TWO POINTS
①
Points A(1,1) and B(5,5). Find distance.
②
Differences:
Δx = 5−1 = 4
Δy = 5−1 = 4
③
Distance formula:
d = √(Δx² + Δy²)
= √(16 + 16) = √32
④
d = 4√2 ≈ 5.66 units
